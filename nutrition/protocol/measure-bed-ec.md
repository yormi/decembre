# Protocol — Bed EC (1:1 by weight)

Official method for bed salinity. All beds, all crops.


## Kit (keep staged in one bin)

- Digital kitchen scale (1 g)

- 6 marked cups + spoon

- Squeeze bottle of water (tap is fine — ~0.10 mS/cm)

- Bluelab EC pen + calibration solution

- Log sheet


## Once per session

- Calibrate pen with calibration solution → rinse.


## Once per season

- Check water EC. >~0.15 mS/cm → subtract from readings; tap (~0.10) → ignore.


## Per round (batch all beds at once)

1. Each bed: 5 cores at root-zone depth (~10 cm) → combine in its cup, skip obvious stones/roots.

2. Scoop soil into the cup → note its weight.

3. Add the same weight of water

4. Stir each ~60 s.

5. One timer → whole batch stands 1 min.

6. Read each → rinse pen between cups → record EC_slurry.


## Record

- Date, bed ID, EC_slurry (mS/cm).


## Canonical value

- Bed salinity = **EC_slurry**.

- Pore-water (model use only) = EC_slurry × 4.

- ±20% with soil wetness — win the method's other uncertainties (e.g. the ×4 pore-water factor)


## Notes

- Tap (~0.10 mS/cm) is clean enough to ignore; no blank needed. Re-check water EC each season.
