# Bed grid readings — salanova, ~2026-06-13

Screenshot of the spreadsheet grid of per-bed readings, six beds
(A–D Nord, E Sud, F Sans lumière). Rows: transplant date, **EC 1:1
with the Pulse** (slurry), **EC peak in-soil** (Pulse stuck in the
bed), pH 1:1, soil temperature.

The numbers are versioned here as the source of truth; the table is
not transcribed into any spec.

**Reach for these when:**

- pulling the raw bed EC/pH/temp figures behind any salanova
  salinity read or the setpoint-band calibration.

- checking which bed runs hot / salty / high-pH (bed A: 28 °C +
  highest slurry EC 3.56; bed E Sud: pH 7.8).

- revisiting the Pulse-vs-1:1 reconciliation — see the learning
  `../../learnings/pulse-in-soil-tracks-moisture-not-salt.md`.

⚠️ The two EC rows are **different scales, not interconvertible**.
The in-soil Pulse row tracks soil moisture as much as salt; the 1:1
slurry row is the moisture-standardised salinity read. Across the
six beds the two rows ~anti-correlate (r ≈ −0.36) — that is the
whole point of the paired learning, not noise.

⚠️ Reading date approximate (~12–13 June); transplant dates in the
grid are exact (17 mai – 10 juin).
