# Lettuce field fertigation — standing recipe (low-K, set-and-forget)

Self-contained handoff sheet. Salanova field beds, organic
(CAN/CGSB-32.311). Tissue-led, low-EC recipe for a salt-stressed crop.

Scale basis: **3 600 heads** (800/bed × 4.5 beds), **30 → 100 g/head**,
**3-week** growth, **54.7 m²** planted, **weekly** batch.


## Recipe (weekly)

| Product | Weekly amount |
|---|---|
| Sulfate de potassium 0-0-50 (K₂SO₄) | **200 g** |
| Sulfate de magnésium heptahydraté (MgSO₄·7H₂O) | **50 g** |
| Sulfate de fer 20% (FeSO₄) | **15 g** |
| Sulfate de manganèse 32% (MnSO₄) | **5 g** |
| Sulfate de zinc 35% (ZnSO₄) | **2.7 g** |
| Borax 15% | **6 g** |
| Sulfate de cuivre 25% (CuSO₄) | **1 g** |

All sulfate forms — also supply S (tissue S was deficient).


## Water + batch

- Apply **~2 080 L/week** of dilute solution (~38 L/m²/wk).
- Weekly stock: **~42 L** injected at **2%**.
- 4-day batch variant: ~24 L stock → ~1 190 L over 4 days, then
  plain water the rest of the week (crop needs the full ~2 080 L/wk).
- Target **20–30% drainage** (leaching) — flushes accumulated sodium.
- Dripper EC ≈ **0.4 mS/cm** added over source water.


## Deliberately excluded

- **Calcium / Phosphorus** — soil banks are large; P is pH-locked.
  Not dosed here; addressed by the soil-pH program, not the tank.
- **Nitrogen** — supplied by feather-meal front-load (pre-plant), not
  this tank.
- **Sodium molybdate** — soil Mo in-spec; adds sodium; dropped.
- **Citric acid** — no benefit on clean, mildly acidic water with a
  fresh batch; dropped.
- **Foliar magnesium** — handled separately, not part of this sheet.


## Notes

- Not a locked spec; no stored lettuce fertigation channel exists yet.
- K is intentionally below weekly removal — the soil K reserve
  (645 kg/ha, ~17-week runway) buffers it; keeps EC low on a
  salt-stressed crop. Re-check at the next tissue/soil test; raise
  toward replacement (~700 g K₂SO₄) only if the bank or tissue K drops.
- Confidence: removal C3 (literature-anchored demand model), product
  conversions C5, area C2 (54.7 m² assumed; doses scale with area).
- All products organic-allowed under CAN/CGSB-32.311 at these rates.
