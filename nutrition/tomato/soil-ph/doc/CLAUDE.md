  source and an alkali-soil amendment; Canadian distributor Tiger-Sul
  (Irricana, AB). Reach for this when:
  - confirming the product's S analysis / particle form behind the
    derivation's 90%-S + bentonite assumption (note: the numeric
    spec table renders as a graphic, not extractable text — open the
    PDF to read it);
  - checking handling/storage constraints (anti-dust agent, keep away
    from oxidisers like ammonium nitrate, avoid auger fracturing);
  - sourcing the Canadian supplier contact.
  - **Cert status UNVERIFIED (open item):** "Organic" is the brand
    name; this sheet carries NO OMRI / Ecocert / CAN-CGSB-32.311
    listing number. Do not treat as proof of organic approval — pull
    the actual OMRI/Ecocert certificate to clear the derivation's
    cert-0 product flag.
