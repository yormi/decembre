### `PXL_20260521_172613938.jpg`
Mid-canopy leaflet, palm-held against white plastic mulch. Interveinal chlorosis on the basal half — yellowing between veins with veins still green; apex stays fully green. Classic mobile-element interveinal pattern on a mid-aged leaf.

### `PXL_20260521_172634558.jpg`
Severe interveinal necrosis + purple-bronze speckling across the whole leaflet, with neighbouring leaflets on the same compound leaf showing the same pattern at different intensities. Veins largely green; necrotic spots concentrated in interveinal tissue. Most striking of the five photos.

### `PXL_20260521_172934654.jpg`
Lower / mid-canopy leaflet. Bright yellow marginal chlorosis along the apex and one side; mid-blade still green; tip starting to brown. Marginal-burn pattern.

### `PXL_20260521_172940425.jpg`
Single leaflet with a tan necrotic blotch (~30 % of blade area) surrounded by a chlorotic halo on the right margin, plus smaller interveinal lesions left of midrib. Other leaves visible in the background show similar marginal yellowing.

### `PXL_20260521_172950014.jpg`
Fully senescing lower leaf next to a ripening fruit truss (green + red fruit visible). Most of the blade is bright yellow with brown necrotic streaks along midrib and margins; green tissue retained only along the central veins of the basal leaflets. Lower-leaf mobilization pattern.
