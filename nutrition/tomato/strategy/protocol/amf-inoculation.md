# Protocol — AMF inoculation (SG1 interim P-uptake bridge)

Deliver P by direct mycorrhizal uptake while the sulphur drawdown
slowly frees the soil P vault. Mechanism + 4/5 rating:
`../../tomato/learnings/0002-interim-root-uptake-levers-during-ph-wait.md`.

**On-hand:** AGTIV REACH **P** (Premier Tech) — peat powder, 8 000 spores/g,
*Rhizophagus irregularis* PTB297. 800 g pail (multi-year).

## Drench — per pass

**Dose**
- Whole block ~1070 plants: **~37 g REACH P/pass**
- Practical mix: **~3 level teaspoons per 20 L bucket**, ~2 buckets/bed/pass

**Steps**
1. Fill 20L bucket water.
2. Add powder → **stir hard, keep agitating** (peat settles, won't dissolve).
3. Pour 1 bucket on one side of the plant row, 1 bucket on the other side. 
4. Scratch surface lightly → carries powder down to feeder roots AND triggers a new-root flush. Shallow only; don't sever the standing mat. Cutting shallow roots will generate root growth that will be colonized by the spores.
5. 5min irrigation to settle in.

## Cadence

- **5 passes, every 10–14 days** — run the remaining vegetative-root window; each catches a fresh flush (especially with the Ocean pulse driving them).

## Guardrails

- **No drip.** REACH P peat powder clogs emitters — hand-drench only.
- **No heavy soluble-P drench in the same window** — raising tissue P represses the mycorrhizal P pathway (the whole mechanism).
- **Ocean fertigation (SG2): keep running.** 1 % P, moderate organic amino-N, microbiome-friendly → complementary (grows root = more surface to colonise). Only avoid flushing the inoculum at the placement moment; resume daily fertigation after.
- **No fungicide drench** near application — kills the inoculant.
- **Soil temp >15 °C** for colonisation.

## Monitoring

- Judge on **tissue P + new-growth vigour**, never a re-tested SME available-P number (AMF raises *tissue* P, not bulk soil P → SME stays flat until pH drops; flat SME ≠ failure).
- AMF + sulphur drawdown complementary: AMF scavenges the available fraction harder now; sulphur enlarges that fraction later.

## Next-transplant reserve (higher odds, ~75 %)

- Single placement at the next tomato transplant — REACH P as a root-coat in direct plug/root contact in the planting hole.
- Rate ~7 g for the full block (~1070 plants; 800 g pail → ~117 000 transplants). One pass — colonisation is permanent + self-spreading; re-dosing a colonised plant is wasted.
- Same cert gate + guardrails.
