# Interim root-uptake levers during the sulphur pH-correction wait

**Date:** 2026-05-31
**Status:** accepted — lever ratings + monitoring reframe; product cert checks still open

The bottleneck is root nutrient-uptake capacity, not bulk pH
(which only the multi-month elemental-sulphur programme moves).
Question: what organic-allowed levers improve uptake / tissue
recovery *this crop, during the wait*? Backed by a deep-research
pass (23/25 claims survived 3-vote adversarial verification);
sources at bottom. Cross-ref [[supply-vs-uptake-cation-test-rules-out-antagonism]],
[[carbonate-gates-ph-correction-feasibility]], `nutrition/strategy/goal.md`.

## Ratings (1–5 = expected impact on tissue recovery this crop)

| Lever | Rating | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Protein-hydrolysate **root drench** (Ocean fertigated) | **5** | tomato substrate-drench → root DW +21%, biomass +17%, SPAD +7%; **drench beats foliar** (roots take up amino-N directly via AAT1). Fast, on-hand, cert-cleared. cert 3 (peat-substrate trials, not our field soil). |
| Mycorrhizae (AMF) | **4** | matched MAP P-fertiliser for field tomato biomass/nutrition; high TOTAL soil P does NOT suppress it here — colonisation is repressed by *plant tissue* P (deficient, leaf 0.23%), not soil P (Nagy 2009 tomato). cert 3 (high-pH transfer extrapolated). |

## The monitoring reframe (load-bearing)

The top levers raise tissue P + vigour by **direct plant uptake**,
NOT by raising bulk-soil available P. Two independent primaries
found soil available P **unchanged** (AMF: Watts-Williams 2021;
fulvic: Frontiers 2021). **Judge these on leaf-tissue P + new-growth
vigour, never on a re-tested SME available-P number** — SME will
not move until sulphur lowers bulk pH. A flat SME ≠ failure.

## Recommended stack (all organic; complementary, not rival)

1. **Ocean as a root drench** (5) — start here; fastest, no new product.
2. **AMF inoculant** (4) — durable; best at any replant, partial mid-crop.
3. **Fulvic drench** (3) — cheap vigour adjunct, stacks.

PSB only if paired with an organic P source (bone meal / rock
phosphate). Crop-load + warming unproven — cheap to try, no evidence backing.

## Cert gate (OPEN)

AMF / PSB / fulvic-humic classes are generally CAN/CGSB-32.311-
compatible but **every specific product needs its Ecocert / OMRI
listing confirmed before use** — do not assume. Ocean (EZ-GRO
15-1-1) already cleared + on hand.
