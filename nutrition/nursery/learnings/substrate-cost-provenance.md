## OM2 price — $0.32 CAD / usable L

- Berger OM2 bale = **3.8 cu ft compressed = ~108 L** (`3.8 × 28.3`).

- **$35 CAD/bale** = single-retail basis. US retail spans $16.50–25.50
  USD ($22–35 CAD). Local Québec pallet (30-bale, e.g. Téris Laval) is
  typically lower → real $/L may run **~$0.19–0.28**.

- **Cert low — not Décembre's invoice.** Replace with the actual
  invoiced $/bale ÷ 108 when on hand. (Asked 2026-06-20; $35 given as
  the working figure.)

- **Fluff caveat:** compressed bales loosen to more usable fill →
  $/L runs a bit *lower* than the 108 L compressed basis.
