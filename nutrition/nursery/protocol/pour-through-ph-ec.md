# Protocol — pour-through pH + EC


## Steps

1. Saturate tray → irrigate until few drips drain.

2. Set tray on rack over clean catch tray.

3. Pour tap water on surface → collect 3 tablespoon pooled leachate.

4. Read pH within minutes → swirl, read, rinse pen.

5. Read leachate EC


## Error band

| Measurement | Tool | Error band |
|---|---|---|
| Pour-through pH | pH pen |  ±0.2 |
| Pour-through EC | liquid EC meter |  −10..−20% (reads low) + ±0.1 |


## Tap Water Correction

- EC accept band = domain root-zone EC band + 0.10 mS/cm (tap water adds 0.10). Compare the raw reading to that; never subtract.

- pH band: no correction — skip-wait pull (−) and tap alkalinity (+) cancel within pen noise.
