# Protocol — Pulse media EC


## Applies to

3" pots / larger cells only. **Not 50-cell trays** — prongs don't seat, no reading (observed 2026-06-13). Use pour-through there.


## Steps

1. Insert Bluelab Pulse prongs fully in representative cell.

2. Sample 5–8 spots across the tray.

3. Record average


## Bands — lettuce seedlings

| Measurement | Tool | Error band |
|---|---|---|
| Media EC | Pulse |  ±~15% (trend only) |
