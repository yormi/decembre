# Domain — nursery


## Model


### Germination conditions

- soil temperature **18–21 °C** → viable germination

- **> 24 °C → dormancy** (thermo-inhibition; seed sits, won't fire)

- **no fertilizer** during germination → **EC < 1 mS/cm** (water-only; young radicle salt-sensitive)


### Container & substrate

- one head = one cell/pot → **containers needed = head count**

- container size → **root volume per head** → substrate volume per head → substrate cost

- substrate cost ← substrate volume **×** mix price   (volume is the binding term; price is one knob)

- container size also drives → **bench area per head**, **fill labor per head**, **root headroom → time-to-transplant**

- the plug → pot jump dominates: 50→32-cell only doubles volume; 32-cell → 3″ pot near-quadruples again
